Administrative and Faculty Space

The faculty and support staff for the Department of Engineering Fundamentals are housed on the first floor of the Lehman Engineering and Technology Center. This office suite opens at 8 a.m. and closes at 5 p.m.

Educational and Research Laboratories

Each course in the Engineering Fundamentals curriculum (EGR 101, EGR 115, and EGR 120) has a designated laboratory space for use as a classroom and for projects related to the course. These spaces are frequently equipped with tutors and available for supervised use.
