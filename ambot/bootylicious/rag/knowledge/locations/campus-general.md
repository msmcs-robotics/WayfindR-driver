# Campus General Locations

General campus locations at ERAU Daytona Beach that are not within a specific engineering building.

## Jack R. Hunt Memorial Library
- **also known as**: Library, Hunt Library, Hazy Library, Main Library
- **floor**: 1

The main library on campus providing study spaces, research resources, computer labs, and quiet study areas. Open to all students and faculty. Houses extensive aviation and aerospace reference collections.

## Student Union Building
- **also known as**: Student Union, SUB, Student Center
- **floor**: 1

The central hub for student life on campus. Contains dining options, meeting rooms, student organization offices, and event spaces. A common gathering place between classes.

## Visitor Center
- **also known as**: Welcome Center, Admissions Office, Admissions Building
- **floor**: 1

The main visitor and admissions center for prospective students and campus visitors. Provides campus tours, admissions information, and a starting point for exploring ERAU.
