# MicaPlex

The MicaPlex is a research-focused building at ERAU's Daytona Beach campus housing advanced laboratories for multiple engineering departments. It provides space for cutting-edge research in robotics, dynamics, spacecraft systems, and materials science.

## Circuits, Sensor and Instrumentation Laboratory (MicaPlex)
- **also known as**: CSIL MicaPlex
- **floor**: 1

A satellite location of the CSIL lab supporting circuit design, fabrication, component testing, and controlled sensor characterization for advanced research projects.

## Computational Sciences Laboratory
- **also known as**: Comp Sci Lab, Computing Lab
- **floor**: 1

High-performance computing resources for computational research in engineering simulations, data analysis, and scientific computing applications.

## Robotics and Autonomous Systems Laboratory (MicaPlex)
- **also known as**: RASL MicaPlex, Robotics Lab MicaPlex
- **floor**: 1

A secondary location for robotics and autonomous systems research, providing additional space for larger robotic platforms and testing environments.

## Advanced Dynamics and Control Research Laboratory
- **also known as**: ADCL, Dynamics Lab
- **floor**: 1

Supports research aimed at advancing aviation and space technologies through dynamic systems modeling, control theory, and experimental validation.

## Astrodynamics and Spacecraft Laboratory
- **also known as**: Spacecraft Lab, Astro Lab
- **floor**: 1

Provides a ground-based hardware-in-the-loop test facility with spacecraft simulators to verify guidance, navigation and control algorithms for spacecraft relative motion and proximity operations.
