# College of Engineering Building

The College of Engineering Building houses administrative offices, advising services, and common spaces for the College of Engineering at ERAU's Daytona Beach campus.

## Dean's Office
- **also known as**: CoE Dean, Engineering Dean's Office, Dean of Engineering
- **floor**: 3
- **room**: 300
<!-- Per ERAU website: CoE administration is in Lehman Suite 300 (3rd floor). This may refer to the same physical location listed under Lehman. -->

The office of the Dean of the College of Engineering. Located in Lehman Engineering and Technology Center, Suite 300, third floor. Handles college-wide administration, strategic initiatives, and inter-departmental coordination.

## Student Success Center
- **also known as**: Advising Office, Academic Advising, Student Advising
- **floor**: 1
<!-- Room number unknown -->

Academic advising and student success services for all College of Engineering students. Advisors help with course selection, degree planning, and academic support resources.

## Main Lobby
- **also known as**: CoE Lobby, Engineering Lobby, Engineering Building Lobby
- **floor**: 1

The main entrance and gathering space for the College of Engineering. Information displays, event postings, and a welcome area for visitors.
