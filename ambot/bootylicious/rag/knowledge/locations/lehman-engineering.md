# Lehman Engineering and Technology Center

The Lehman Engineering and Technology Center is the primary engineering building at ERAU's Daytona Beach campus. It houses the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science on the third floor, along with numerous research and teaching laboratories. The building faces Clyde Morris Boulevard on its east side.
