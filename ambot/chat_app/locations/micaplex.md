# MicaPlex

## Circuits, Sensor and Instrumentation Laboratory
- **aliases**: CSIL Lab
- **building**: MicaPlex
- **floor**: 1

The Circuits, Sensor and Instrumentation Laboratory provides hands-on experience with electronic circuit design, sensor integration, and measurement instrumentation. Students work with oscilloscopes, signal generators, and a wide range of sensors used in aerospace and engineering applications.

## Computational Sciences Laboratory
- **building**: MicaPlex
- **floor**: 1

The Computational Sciences Laboratory supports coursework and research in numerical methods, scientific computing, and high-performance simulation. Equipped with workstations for computationally intensive tasks, this lab serves students across multiple engineering disciplines.

## Robotics and Autonomous Systems Laboratory (MicaPlex)
- **aliases**: RASL MicaPlex
- **building**: MicaPlex
- **floor**: 1

The MicaPlex location of the Robotics and Autonomous Systems Laboratory provides additional space for robotics research and development beyond the main RASL in the Lehman building. This facility focuses on autonomous vehicle testing and multi-robot systems experimentation.

## Advanced Dynamics and Control Research Laboratory
- **aliases**: ADCL
- **building**: MicaPlex
- **floor**: 1

The Advanced Dynamics and Control Research Laboratory conducts research in nonlinear dynamics, control systems design, and stability analysis for aerospace and mechanical systems. Graduate and undergraduate students collaborate here on projects involving spacecraft attitude control, vibration analysis, and adaptive control algorithms.

## Astrodynamics and Spacecraft Laboratory
- **building**: MicaPlex
- **floor**: 1

The Astrodynamics and Spacecraft Laboratory focuses on orbital mechanics, spacecraft mission design, and satellite systems engineering. Researchers here work on trajectory optimization, constellation design, and spacecraft subsystem prototyping for CubeSat and small satellite missions.
