# Lehman Engineering and Technology Center

## EECS Department Office
- **aliases**: EECS Office, Department Office, 340 Suite
- **building**: Lehman Engineering and Technology Center
- **floor**: 3
- **room**: 340

The Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science main office suite is located on the third floor of the Lehman Engineering and Technology Center, in the 340 suite on the east side of the building facing Clyde Morris Boulevard. This is where you'll find the department chair and administrative staff.

## Robotics and Autonomous Systems Laboratory
- **aliases**: RASL, Robotics Lab
- **building**: Lehman Engineering and Technology Center
- **floor**: 3

The Robotics and Autonomous Systems Laboratory (RASL) supports research activities into the design and operation of uncrewed and automated systems. This is where the AMBOT project is developed and tested.

## Cybersecurity Engineering Laboratory
- **aliases**: Cyber Lab, Cybersecurity Lab
- **building**: Lehman Engineering and Technology Center
- **floor**: 3

Furnished with state-of-the-art hardware and software to permit participation in cybersecurity attack and defend exercises. Students work on hands-on cybersecurity projects here.

## Digital Systems Laboratory
- **aliases**: Digital Lab, FPGA Lab
- **building**: Lehman Engineering and Technology Center
- **floor**: 3

Real-time embedded software development and research in digital electronics, microcontrollers and field-programmable gate arrays (FPGAs) are performed in the Digital Systems Laboratory.

## EECS Capstone Design Laboratory
- **aliases**: Capstone Lab, Senior Design Lab
- **building**: Lehman Engineering and Technology Center
- **floor**: 3

Students in the EECS programs work on projects for their multidisciplinary capstone design courses here. This is where senior design teams build and test their final projects.

## Software Development Lab
- **aliases**: Software Lab, CS Lab
- **building**: Lehman Engineering and Technology Center
- **floor**: 3

The Software Development Lab provides a multidisciplinary environment for instruction and experiential learning in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering and Aerospace Engineering software development.

## Engineering Student Projects Center
- **aliases**: ESPC, Student Projects, Maker Space
- **building**: Lehman Engineering and Technology Center

Hands-on exploration and fabrication of engineering designs and solutions to problems posed in the Engineering Fundamentals core curriculum. Students come here to build physical prototypes and work on engineering projects.

## Speech and Language AI Lab
- **aliases**: SaLAI Lab, NLP Lab
- **building**: Lehman Engineering and Technology Center
- **floor**: 3

Advancing speech and language AI research at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. Research focuses on natural language processing, speech recognition, and conversational AI systems.
