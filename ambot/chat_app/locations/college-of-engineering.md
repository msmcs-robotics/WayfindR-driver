# College of Engineering

## Dean's Office
- **aliases**: CoE Dean, Engineering Dean's Office
- **building**: College of Engineering Building
- **floor**: 2

The Dean's Office for the College of Engineering is located on the second floor of the College of Engineering Building. This is where the Dean, Associate Deans, and college-level administrative staff manage academic programs, faculty affairs, and strategic initiatives for all engineering departments.

## Student Success Center
- **aliases**: Advising Office
- **building**: College of Engineering Building
- **floor**: 1

The Student Success Center provides academic advising, degree planning, and student support services for all College of Engineering students. Advisors here help with course registration, degree audits, and career guidance to keep students on track toward graduation.

## Main Lobby
- **aliases**: CoE Lobby, Engineering Lobby
- **building**: College of Engineering Building
- **floor**: 1

The main lobby of the College of Engineering Building serves as a central gathering point for engineering students and visitors. The space features displays showcasing student projects, research achievements, and information about the college's academic programs.
