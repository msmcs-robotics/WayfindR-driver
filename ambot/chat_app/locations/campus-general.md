# Campus General

## Library
- **aliases**: Hunt Library, Hazy Library
- **building**: Jack R. Hunt Memorial Library

The Jack R. Hunt Memorial Library is the main academic library on the Daytona Beach campus, offering study spaces, research databases, and an extensive collection of aviation, aerospace, and engineering resources. The library also provides tutoring services, group study rooms, and access to interlibrary loan materials.

## Student Union
- **aliases**: SUB
- **building**: Student Union Building

The Student Union Building is the hub of campus life at ERAU Daytona Beach, housing dining options, student organization offices, and event spaces. Students gather here between classes to eat, socialize, and participate in club activities and campus programming.

## Visitor Center
- **aliases**: Welcome Center
- **building**: Admissions Building

The Visitor Center in the Admissions Building is the starting point for campus tours and prospective student visits. Admissions counselors are available here to answer questions about programs, financial aid, and the application process at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University.
